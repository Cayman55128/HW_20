Problem Statement

Family members and advocates of those afflicted by addiction desire recovery programs that they can trust and have high rates of success. Currently, the TCRP website does not offer enough insight into these areas in an accessible way for our users which means the program is suffering financially and potential candidates of the program go unaided.

Solution

The solution for the redesign of the Twin Cities Recovery Program website is to provide family members and advocates of those afflicted by addiction with easy access to evaluate and find the recovery programs that TCRP offers, addressing the current lack of transparency and accessibility, which in turn could help improve the program's financial situation and support more candidates.
